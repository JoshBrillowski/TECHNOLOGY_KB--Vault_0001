###### Figma > Navigation > **Zooming**
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
<br>

- ###### Zooming In and Out:
  - `Cmd + [PlusKey | MinuKey]`
  - `Cmd + ScrollWheel`

- ###### Zoom to Size:
  - `Shft + 0` --> **Zoom to Actual Size (100%)**
  - `Shft + 1` --> **Zoom to Fit**
